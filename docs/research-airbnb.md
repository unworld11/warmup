## What they actually post (TikTok)

Their current feed is almost entirely a **FIFA World Cup 2026 campaign**, in a few recurring formats:

- **Host-city travel guides** — where to watch outdoors, local-favourite spots, what to eat near the action (e.g. Wimbledon Village, "outdoor screens are up, grab a picnic blanket").
- **Creator series** — an ongoing episodic show following creators city to city, plus partner creators (a motorcycle-tour guide, an SNL cast member "staying with Airbnb").
- **National-culture love letters** — short odes to host nations (Mexico, Norway, Spain, Argentina, Canada, Morocco, Portugal) framed around hospitality and "being a great host."
- **Belonging / "hosts who feel like family"** — the throughline under all of it.

Voice: warm, witty, culturally rooted. Not salesy.

## The engagement pattern (the real insight)

- Culture/emotion posts **travel**: a host-nation love letter hit **~7.7M** plays; a flagship creator episode **~6.7M**; a "champions of aggressive friendliness" clip **~1.4M**.
- Campaign-mechanic posts **stall**: ticket drops, bracket updates and announcements mostly sit at **2–4K** plays.
- Read: they make culture-tier creative and get follower-tier reach on the majority of it. The lever is **distribution + volume of the stuff that already resonates**, not "better content."

Real intent, real sentiment, thousands of readers — **and the brand is absent from all of it.**
